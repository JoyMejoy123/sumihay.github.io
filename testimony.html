Testimony

  • I am Joy M. Sumihay writting my testimony about this training of Empowering Community ALS Implementer thru ICT. This testimony express my utmost admiration to the staff of Northern Bukidnon State College especially to the trainers/teachers in the College of Computer Studies for making this training possible. As one of the Community ALS Implementer of Manolo Fortich District III it is a big help to widen and boaden my learning in terms of technology, I will use this knowledge to enhance ang equipped my learners in the future. During the learning process I have learned so many things like, knowing the hardware, software, front and back panel of a System Unit which the trainer/teacher explain to us very will the detailed and what are the specific funtions of each devices. I have learned also about the Familiarizing the some of the shortcut keys and exploring the function and uses of spreedsheets and how to use the different formulas in the Microsoft Excel in which the teacher was very hands on in teaching us the different techniques. I am very lucky and thankful to be part of this trainings because I have learned so many information that I can use in my future works. And I am very thankful to the people behind all this trainings for putting so much efforts to make this trainings possible.
